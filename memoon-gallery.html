Design Decisions Log
Color Palette
Gold
Red
Aqua
Brown
Cream
Inspiration
Italian Riviera 1960s. Dolce Vita. ELLE magazine cover. Slim Aarons. The dress in the pool photo.
Typography
Georgia serif — all-caps spaced for modules, meta, UI. Reenie Beanie — handwriting for "your solo travel companion" only.
Letter Colors — Final
M E M O O N
white · red · black · white · red · black
Content — 25 Videos
Start Here (7) · Confidence (6) · Emotional Support (8) · Transformation (2) · Bonus + Missions (2)
Tech Stack
PWA · Single HTML file · Vimeo for video hosting · Stripe for payments · GitHub + Vercel for deploy
Pricing Strategy
$67 founders price → $97 after launch. One-time payment, no subscription for v1.
Key UX Decisions
No price on app home screen. Quote on photo only, not repeated. White handwritten arrow as pointer. Swipe carousel saves vertical space.
